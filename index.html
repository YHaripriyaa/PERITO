Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

What response time can I expect for support requests?

Our standard response time is within 2 hours for priority requests and within 24 hours for standard requests. We offer 24/7 support for critical issues with guaranteed SLAs based on your service level agreement.

Do you provide onsite support or remote support?

We provide both onsite and remote support depending on your requirements. Most issues can be resolved remotely, but we dispatch certified engineers onsite when necessary. Our hybrid approach ensures maximum efficiency and minimal downtime.

What certifications do your engineers have?

All our engineers have necessary technical certifications including Microsoft, Cisco, Red Hat, AWS, and other relevant certifications based on their specialization. We also provide continuous training to keep our team updated with the latest technologies.

How do you ensure data security and privacy?

We are ISO/IEC 27001:2013 certified and follow strict security protocols. All our engineers are background verified and sign comprehensive NDAs. We implement end-to-end encryption and comply with all data protection regulations relevant to your industry.
